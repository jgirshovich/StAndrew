A Saint Andrew Education

Three Pillars of Our Mission

Spiritual Formation

Our Catholic identity is not an add-on — it's the foundation. Daily Mass, prayer, and service learning form students' hearts as deeply as their minds.

Academic Excellence

A rigorous, standards-exceeding curriculum that prepares every student for the demands of high school and the opportunities of a lifetime.

Community & Belonging

We are a parish school — a true family. Relationships between students, teachers, and families are built on mutual respect, care, and shared faith.
