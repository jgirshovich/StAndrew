Welcome to Saint Andrew

A Faith-Filled Community Where Students Thrive

We are a parish school in the heart of Coral Springs, Florida, offering a full Catholic education from Preschool through 8th Grade. Our students are known by name, loved by their teachers, and challenged to become the people God created them to be.

"Saint Andrew Catholic School prepares students in a spiritual, academic and nurturing environment to become productive leaders rooted in the Catholic virtues of faith, hope, and love."

Our motto — First Called — reflects the example of Saint Andrew himself, the first disciple to answer Christ's call. We invite every family to discover what that calling looks like for your child.
